Purpose

The website will highlight the exciting experiences aboard a cruise ship excursion. It will showcase the entertainment, food, and activities that are available while on board the cruise ship. Finally, it allows the visitor to see all the possible destinations.

Audience

Visitors the website will be travelers seeking a vacation experience that takes them beyond a single destination - exploring multiple countries and cultures along the way.

Content

Home page

Cruising Experience

The cruise is an amazing opportunity for families to make new memories. Whether you are looking for adventure, relaxation, or non-stop fun, cruise ships offer something for everyone. The destinations range from tropical beaches to historical locations.

Dining Experience

Mealtimes become fantastic family moments as everyone discovers a world of flavors. From fresh seafood to kid-friendly favorites, thereâ€™s something to satisfy every appetite. The buffet opens early and closes late to allow guests to dine at any given time. Reservations are available for guests that desire a high-quality restaurant experience. And make sure to leave room for dessert as our kitchens provide ice cream treats, pastries and cookies throughout the day.

Activities and Entertainment

During the day, passengers can enjoy swimming pools, water slides, and sun decks for relaxing or soaking in the ocean views. Fitness centers, sports courts and jogging tracks are available for those who like to stay active. Spas provide massages, saunas and beauty treatments while passengers relax. Evenings bring live entertainment, including Broadway-style shows, comedy acts, movies under the stars and dance parties.

Activites

Water Activities

For guests that want to spend time in the water, there are infinity pools and swimming pools available on the upper deck. Adventurous guests will enjoy the waterslides that will cork screw and drop from the upper deck to the lower deck. While younger guests will enjoy time in the splash zones.

Sports Activities

An enclosed sport arena allows guest to play basketball, pickleball and rock climbing. In adjacent areas, guests can play mini-golf and ping-pong.

Relaxation Activities

An adult only retreat allows guests to relax in the spa and receive massages. Also included is an area for yoga, Pilates and dance classes

Live Entertainment

Each night multiple shows will entertain guests with singers, dance performances, comedians and magicians. The upper deck hosts a different themed dance party each night with live DJs.

Shopping Activities

While on board, guests can discover many stores that are filled with jewelry and perfumes. Souvenir shops will include items that are specific to the destination of the cruise.

Shore Excursions

There are many shore excursions available to guests while the ship is docked. The excursions will include exploration of the area, shopping opportunities, and fun-filled activities that are specific for the area.

Destinations

From sun-soaked tropical islands to glacier carved fjords, every cruise offers its own adventure, beauty and culture. Whether you are seeking relaxation, exploration or adventure, there are many destinations to discover.

Caribbeans

The Caribbean cruises offer crystal clear waters and white sand beaches with lots of sunshine. On the eastern tour, guests will visit the Virgin Islands, St. Maarten and Puerto Rico. On the western tour, guests explore Cozumel, Grand Cayman, and Jamaica.

Hawaii

The Hawaiian cruises offer a variety of tropical landscapes and lush valleys. Guests can visit the many waterfalls on Maui or the black-sand beaches on the Big Island. The Polynesian Culture Center on Oahu offers a full day of activities while demonstrating the life styles of the people in the area.

Mexico

A Mexican cruise offers guests the opportunity to explore ancient culture and coastal beauty. Excursions include tours of the Mayan ruins and visits to cenotes deep within the jungles.

Alaska

Discover a new rugged land covered with glaciers and wilderness on the Alaska cruise. Guests will see whales, bears, eagles within their natural habitat. Ports include Juneau, Skagway and Ketchikan that offer rugged beauty.

Mediterranean

Explore ancient civilizations and see ancient wonders while visiting Rome, Athens, and Barcelona. Discover a variety of foods and cultures within each port.
