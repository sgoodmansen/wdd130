Activities and Entertainment

Water Fun

For guests that want to spend time in the water, there are infinity pools and swimming pools available on the upper deck. Adventurous guests will enjoy the waterslides that will cork screw and drop from the upper deck to the lower deck. While younger guests will enjoy time in the splash zones.

Sports and Games

We offer an exciting lineup of sports activities for all ages, from basketball courts and jogging tracks to mini-golf, pickleball, and state-of-the-art fitness centers. Guests can enjoy friendly competitions, ocean-view workouts, and energizing outdoor challenges, creating the perfect mix of fun, fitness, and adventure at sea.

Relaxation

Our spas provide a serene escape with soothing massages and aromatherapy treatments. Guests can unwind in tranquil lounges, enjoy ocean-view saunas, and embrace mindful relaxation. From rejuvenating facials to peaceful poolside retreats, every moment is designed elevate your sense of calm at sea.

Live Entertainment

Cruise ships come alive each night with dazzling entertainment, from Broadway-style shows and immersive theatrical performances to live music, comedy acts, and vibrant themed parties. Guests can enjoy world-class talent in stunning venues, creating unforgettable evenings filled with energy, excitement, and captivating performances for every taste.

Shopping

On board shopping offers a stylish mix of duty-free boutiques, designer brands, and unique souvenirs. Guests can browse fine jewelry, fashion, fragrances, and local artisan goods, all within steps of their stateroom. Shore Excursions

Shore excursions offer unforgettable adventures beyond the ship, from guided cultural tours and historic landmarks to snorkeling, wildlife encounters, and thrilling outdoor activities.
